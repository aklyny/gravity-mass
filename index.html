Welcome!

As per our Axioms, Space is the source of Gravity; all functions and results of the universe emerge from this premise.

Beginning from the year 1999, peer reviewed, published research papers as well as contributors essays are available for viewing.

We have provided a feedback/discussion forum as well – your engagement in the form of questions, clarifications and comments are valued and will be responded to.
